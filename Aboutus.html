This is our founder Sam, Linx was founded in 2010. Sam was an enthusiastic artist who always loved creating pieces. There was just one problem, Sam felt that the prices people were paying for there supplies was too much for what they were getting. No amount of deals, discounts, nor coupons could help, when in the end your still paying a large amount for what your getting. Then one day Sam had an Idea of making his own Company for other people like him. But first the name had to come into play of course. The name Linx is an old family name that has been passed down through generations, Sam decided to continue the tradition, but that would be a lie if we told you that. the real reason that Sam deceided to name the Company Linx, is becuase of how links sounds kind of like Linx, and the way that came about was from Sam's fans always asking for links to where they get there supplies. So that's the real reason we came to be.
